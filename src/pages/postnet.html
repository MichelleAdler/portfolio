Postnet

Mobile App Design

This is a sleek and user-friendly design for a PostNet mobile app, created to streamline courier services, printing requests, and store interactions into one modern digital experience. The goal was to design an intuitive platform that simplifies how users engage with PostNet’s services while reflecting the brand’s reliability and professionalism.

Branding & Style

  • Clean, modern interface with a professional yet approachable feel, using PostNet’s recognizable red accents for brand consistency.
  • Minimalist iconography and clear typography for a straightforward and accessible user experience.
  • Balanced use of white space and intuitive layouts to keep the app light, clean, and easy to navigate.

App Structure & Features

  • Dashboard: Central hub providing quick access to tracking, shipping quotes, and print orders.
  • Parcel Tracking: Real-time updates and streamlined input for tracking numbers.
  • Print & Copy Requests: Simple step-by-step forms to upload documents and choose services before store pickup.
  • Store Locator: Interactive map and store search with operating hours and directions.
  • Notifications: Clean, non-intrusive updates to keep users informed about shipments or ready orders.

Outcome

  • The design enhances PostNet’s service accessibility, putting core actions like tracking and booking at the user’s fingertips.
  • Interactive prototype showcases smooth transitions and a thoughtful user flow that reduces friction in everyday tasks.
  • Overall, the app concept blends utility with clean aesthetics, presenting PostNet as a brand that values both efficiency and customer experience.
