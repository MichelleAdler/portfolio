Hypro Hydraulics

Website, Branding and Logo Design

Hypro Hydraulics is a South African company that provides hydraulic and industrial solutions. I designed their new logo and website to strengthen their online presence and give them a modern, professional brand identity.

Logo

  • Designed a logo that feels reliable and precise while remaining simple enough for versatile use.
  • The final design has an industrial edge but stays clean and adaptable.
  • Gives the brand a sharp, trustworthy look that works across different formats.

Website

  • Showcases Hypro’s core services: hydraulic systems, components, repairs, pneumatics, and safety equipment.
  • Built with a straightforward layout and easy navigation so visitors quickly find what they need.
  • Includes a clear step-by-step process (consultation through to delivery and support) to build customer confidence.
  • Contact information is always accessible, with multiple options including WhatsApp for quick help.

Outcome

  • The new logo and website create a consistent identity that feels professional and approachable.
  • Design speaks directly to industries like construction, mining, transport, and agriculture.
  • Delivers a simple, user-friendly experience while strengthening Hypro’s brand presence.
