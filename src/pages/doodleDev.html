Doodle Dev

Website, Branding and Logo Design

This is a playful and creative website design for a small freelancing studio that offers web development, branding, and graphic design services. The goal was to create a unique, memorable experience that reflects the studio’s fun, approachable personality while showcasing their capabilities.

Branding & Style

  • Used bold, cartoon-inspired characters and illustrations to give the site a friendly and distinctive identity.
  • Applied a bright, modern color palette with high contrast to make key sections and calls-to-action stand out.
  • Typography and layout emphasize clarity while keeping the overall tone lighthearted and creative.

Website Structure & Features

  • Hero Section: Introduces the team with expressive characters and highlights their services (Web Development, Branding, Graphic Design) in a visually engaging way.
  • About Section: Communicates the studio’s philosophy and creative approach, giving visitors insight into the people behind the work.
  • Portfolio / “We Said We Could” Section: A clean and interactive carousel layout to showcase past projects and demonstrate the studio’s skills.
  • Contact / “We Can Chat” Section: Simple form design paired with mascot illustrations to encourage users to get in touch.

Outcome

  • The design reflects the studio’s playful, creative brand while keeping the interface intuitive and user-friendly.
  • Strong visual hierarchy and fun interactions guide visitors naturally from learning about the team to exploring work samples and contacting the studio.
  • Overall, the website balances personality with professionalism, making it both engaging and effective in presenting the studio’s services.
